# 附录 03c：McNamara 谬误 / Kerr (1975) / Surrogation 考证（子代理原始报告摘要）

> 一手核证（Crossref、原文扫描页、档案研究）。三大要点：
> ① 著名的"四步引文"是 **Yankelovich 1971 年演讲**的话，不是 McNamara 的；
> ② Kerr (1975) 的出版信息与原文例子已逐字核实；
> ③ Surrogation 的定义、心理机制（属性替代）与实验发现已核实。

## 1. McNamara 谬误

四步引文（canonical）：
> "The first step is to measure whatever can be easily measured. This is okay as far as it goes. The second step is to disregard that which can't be easily measured or to give it an arbitrary quantitative value. This is artificial and misleading. The third step is to presume that what can't be measured easily really isn't very important. This is blindness. The fourth step is to say that what can't be easily measured really doesn't exist. This is suicide."

出处链（由 Ryan Madden 的档案研究确认）：
- **最早出处：** Daniel Yankelovich, "The New Odds"，1971-10-15 在纽约销售主管俱乐部第 11 届营销战略会议的演讲。
- 一个月后的浓缩版：Yankelovich, "Interpreting the New Life Styles," *Sales Management*, 1971-11-15（"But when the McNamara discipline is applied too literally, the first step is to..."）。
- 常被引用的 *Corporate Priorities* (1972) 是真实出版物，但比演讲晚一年——**建议引 1971 演讲为源头**。
- **Charles Handy 在 *The Empty Raincoat* (1994) 中把这段话误归给 McNamara 本人**（且拼错名字"Macnamara"），是讹传的主要传播载体。

越战语境属实：McNamara（国防部长 1961–68，福特"Whiz Kids"出身）把系统分析引入五角大楼；反叛乱战争没有战线，进展只能靠代理指标——**body count 与 kill ratio**——而这些数字被系统性地灌水、操纵，且无法量化的乡村政治情绪（"x-factor"）被直接丢弃。

## 2. Kerr (1975) "On the Folly of Rewarding A, While Hoping for B"

- *Academy of Management Journal* 18(4): 769–783, DOI 10.5465/255378（JSTOR 遗留 DOI 10.2307/255378）。
- 1995 年重印并附回顾评论：*Academy of Management Executive* 9(1): 7–14, DOI 10.5465/ame.1995.9503133466。

核心论点（原文逐字）：
> "most organisms seek information concerning what activities are rewarded, and then seek to do (or at least pretend to do) those things, often to the virtual exclusion of activities not rewarded."

原文例子：政客的"官方目标 vs 操作目标"；越战奖励结构实际上奖励了抗命（fragging、search and evade）；医生两类错误的不对称惩罚；**大学"希望教学好却几乎只奖励科研发表"**（最常被引用）；孤儿院/康复中心的目标置换；MBO 奖励短期可量化结果却"希望"团队合作与长期投资。

全文扫描：https://www.ou.edu/russell/UGcomp/Kerr.pdf

## 3. Surrogation（管理会计）

定义（2012 摘要逐字）：
> "managers may fail to fully appreciate the fact that measures are merely representations of the strategic constructs, and act as though the measures are the construct of interest—a phenomenon we label surrogation."

文献：
- Choi, Hecht & Tayler (2012), "Lost in Translation: The Effects of Incentive Compensation on Strategy Surrogation," *The Accounting Review* 87(4): 1135–1163, DOI 10.2308/accr-10273。
- Choi, Hecht & Tayler (2013), *Journal of Accounting Research* 51(1): 105–133, DOI 10.1111/j.1475-679X.2012.00465.x。
- 通俗版：Harris & Tayler (2019), "Don't Let Metrics Undermine Your Business," *HBR* 97(5): 62–69 —— 头号案例是 **Wells Fargo**：交叉销售数（"长期客户关系"的代理指标）成为目标 → 约 350 万个未授权账户 → 摧毁了战略本想建立的客户关系。
- 后续：Black, Meservy, Tayler & Williams (2021), *JMAR* 34(1): 9–29；Bentley (2019), *The Accounting Review* 94(3): 27–55（叙事性汇报可减少扭曲与 surrogation）。

机制 = **Kahneman & Frederick 的属性替代（attribute substitution）**：难以评估的抽象构念被容易评估的具体指标无意识替代。关键实验发现：**激励与单一指标挂钩会加剧 surrogation；多指标并用可缓解**（多个测量在暗示"没有哪个数字就是战略本身"）。

与 Goodhart 的关系：**surrogation 是个体层面的认知机制（把指标当构念），Goodhart 是系统层面的后果（指标一旦成为目标就崩坏）**。前者解释了为什么 Goodhart 行为在当事人心里"不觉得是作弊"。
